Ingredients

  • Cooked white rice(day old ideally)
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Chilli peppers
  • kecap manis(sweet soy sauce)

Steps

  1. mix eggs in a bowl
  2. prep the garlic, onions, and peppers
  3. cook and the eggs as though you're making an omlette in high heat
  4. set the eggs aside, and then in the same wok cook the onions and peppers
  5. as soon as the oil becomes fragrent add the garlic
  6. now comes the rice, put your rice in the wok and mixed around
  7. follow it up right after with the egges and mix them
  8. add in the kecap manis(sweet soy sauce) and fry everything till it's done
  9. (OPTIONAL) fry up some sunny side up eggs for more protien
