Aglio Olio

Home

Description

easy, quick and simple pasta dish, add your choice of protien to it and it's a full meal

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Chilli peppers
  • Parsley
  • (OPTIONAL)Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta as per the package instructions
  2. As your pasta is cooking prep your garlic, chilli, and parsley
  3. infuse the garlic and chilli into the olive oil
  4. Add the cooked pasta into the infused oil and add pasta water to it as necessary
  5. Add the parsley to the pasta
  6. plate up, grate some cheese if you have it
